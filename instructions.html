Design and Technology

Overview

On the day:

Each competitor or group will have 5 minutes to present their task.

Task:

Design a solution that allows members to report a problem with a public asset such as road problem, broken streetlight, water leakage, vegetation issue, abandoned vehicle, graffiti, etc. with the following features:

  • Ability for users to upload the photo(s);
  • Ability to capture the geolocation of where the problem is located;
  • Ability to interpret and allocate the classification of the problem through image detection and analysis (optional);
  • Ability to record the user's contact details for further clarifications or acknowledgement; and
  • Back-end system designs that can capture the report and routes to appropriate agent(s) to follow up.
